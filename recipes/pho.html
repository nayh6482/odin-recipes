Pho

Description

Vietnamese noodle soup with various beef cuts

Ingridients

  • Aromatics
  • Spices
  • Beef & beef bone
  • Seasoning
  • Rice noodles
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Heat oil with aromatics until fragrant.
  2. Rinse Beef bones and blanch for five minutes
  3. Add beef, bones, spices, sugar and salt into a pot. Simmer three hours.
  4. Remove brisket and simmer soup uncovered for 40 minutes.
  5. Strain broth, add fish sauce, assemble with noodles and toppings.
