Bubble tea

Description

Milk tea with tapioca pearls

Ingridients

  • Any type of milk
  • Brown sugar
  • 3~4 english breakfast tea bags
  • Tapioca pearl

Steps

  1. Combine water and brown sugar in 1-to-1 ratio in a small pot and boil.
  2. Add tapioca pearls and cook until soft.
  3. Let the pearls cool. Meanwhile, brew tea with 3 or 4 tea bags and 1 cup boiling water.
  4. In a mug of glass, combine pearls, ice chips, tea and milk in order.
  5. Give it a light shake and enjoy!
